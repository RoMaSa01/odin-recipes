Easy Roasted Broccoli

Description

Roasted broccoli is a simple and delicious way to prepare this vegetable. Roasting broccoli in the oven makes it tender on the inside and crispy on the outside, with a slightly roasted and sweet flavor. Roasted broccoli is a perfect accompaniment to many dishes, such as meat, fish, pasta or salads. It can also be eaten alone, with a little lemon and Parmesan cheese, or with your favorite sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 g fresh broccoli
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • Juice and zest of half a lemon
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220ÂºC and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cut the broccoli into small florets and wash them well. Dry them with kitchen paper or a clean cloth.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, salt and pepper. Add the broccoli and stir well so that it is well impregnated with the mixture.
  4. Spread the broccoli on the baking sheet, leaving space between the florets. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender and golden brown around the edges. Halfway through baking, turn the broccoli over so that it is done on both sides.
  5. Remove the broccoli from the oven and sprinkle with the lemon juice and zest. If you like, sprinkle a little Parmesan cheese on top. Serve hot or warm, on its own or with your favorite dish.
