Glazed Carrots

Description

Glazed carrots are an easy and delicious side dish that can be served with any main course. Carrots are cooked in water and then saut√©ed with butter and brown sugar until tender and shiny. Glazed carrots are a dish that appeals to both children and adults, and brings a touch of color and sweetness to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 60 g butter
  • 60 g brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground white pepper
  • Chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Place carrots in a large pot and cover with water. Bring the water to a boil and reduce the heat. Cover the pot and cook the carrots until tender, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the water and transfer the carrots to a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in the same saucepan over low heat. Add the brown sugar, salt and pepper and stir until dissolved. Add the carrots to the pot and stir well to coat them in the butter and sugar mixture. Cook and stir until the carrots are hot and completely glazed, 1 to 3 minutes more.
  3. If desired, sprinkle a little chopped fresh parsley on top of the carrots. Serve hot or warm, on its own or with your favorite dish.
